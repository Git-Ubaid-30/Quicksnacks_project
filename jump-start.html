Jump-start

How to Make Jump-start-smoothie & What it's Benefits

Jump Start smoothies, juices and smoothie bowls are preservative-free, no added sugar, no high fructose corn syrup, and 0 grams trans fat. A healthy and fat laden snacks.

Ingredients / Direction

Ingredients:

► 1 c. frozen strawberries

► 1/2 c. fresh blueberries

► 1/2 c. fresh orange juice

► 2 tsp. chopped and peeled fresh ginger

► 1/4 c. plain low-fat (1-percent) yogurt

Direction:

► In blender, combine strawberries, blueberries, orange juice, ginger, yogurt, and ice cubes.

► Blend until smooth, scraping down side of container occasionally.

Calories:

► 15

Proteins:

► 0.8 grams
