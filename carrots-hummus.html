Carrots-hummus

How to Make Carrots-hummus & What it's Benefits

The fiber content in carrots and chickpeas can help regulate blood sugar levels, for individuals with diabetes or those looking to manage their blood sugar levels.

Ingredients / Direction

Ingredients:

► 500g Carrot - 3 medium to large carrots - chopped

► 1 heaping Cup Onion (135g) - 1/2 of a medium onion - sliced

► 1 Tablespoon Olive oil

► 1/2 Teaspoon Salt

► 1/2 Cup Water

► 1 Can (540ml) of Chickpeas (home cooked chickpeas by weight will be 355g approximately, water NOT included)

► 4 Tablespoon Tahini

► 3 tablespoon Good quality Olive oil (I have used cold pressed extra virgin olive oil)

► 3 to 4 tablespoon Lemon juice OR to taste

► 2 Garlic cloves grated or minced

► 1 Teaspoon Ground cumin

► 1/4 Teaspoon Cayenne pepper (OPTIONAL) OR to taste

► Salt to taste (I have added 1 teaspoon pink Himalayan salt)

TOPPING FOR HUMMUS DIP (Optional):

► Paprika

► Olive oil

► Parsley

Direction:

► Heat a pan and add olive oil, carrots and onion. Cook on high to medium high heat (depending on the heat of your oven) until the carrots and onion starts to caramelize, it may take about 4 to 5 minutes.

► At this point add the water and reduce the heat to low and cook it until the carrots are soft and mushy.

► We want the carrots to be cooked thoroughly it will take about 10 to 12 minutes (depending on the kind of carrots your are using).

► Once the carrots are cooked turn off the heat and let it sit COVERED allowing it to cool down. Its really important that you cover the lid, this will prevent the carrots from drying out (as we want them soft and moist for it to blend properly).

► DO NOT throw away the liquid from the cooked carrot/onion, we will need all of it.

► Now to a food processor add the drained cooked chickpeas, add the cooked carrots & onion (along with any remaining liquid) , tahini, garlic, lemon juice, ground cumin, cayenne, salt and olive oil and blend.

► Blend it to a smooth fluffy puree.

► Make sure to scrape the sides of the container and give it a mix in between so it blends well, it will take about 5 to 6 minutes (depending on your food process).

► The end product should be smooth and airy, so take your time to blend it.

► This is optional but to eat the hummus as a dip sprinkle some paprika, finely chopped parsley and olive oil.

Calories:

► 99

Proteins:

► 3 grams
