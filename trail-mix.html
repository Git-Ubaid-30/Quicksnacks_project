Trail-mix

How to Make Trail-mix & What it's Benefits

Nuts and seeds in trail mix are excellent sources of plant-based protein, which is essential for building and repairing tissues, supporting immune function.

Ingredients / Direction

Ingredients:

► 8-10 cashew nuts, vertically halved

► 10-12 almonds, vertically halved

► 15-20 pistachios

► 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds

► 1 tbsp melon seeds

► 1 tbsp sunflower seeds

► 1 tbsp flax seeds

► 2 tbsps chopped dried coconut

► 2 cups puffed lotus seeds (makhana)

► 2 tbsps oil

► 1 tsp red chilli powder

► ¼ tsp turmeric powder

► ½ tsp chaat masala

► ½ tsp dried mango powder (amchur)

► ½ tsp black salt

► 1 tsp powdered sugar

► 2-3 dried fig slices, chopped

► 20-25 raisins

► ¼ cup dark chocolate chips

Direction:

► Dry roast cashew nuts, almonds, pistachios, pumpkin seeds, melon seeds, sunflower seeds, and flax seeds in a non-stick pan for 3-4 minutes on low heat, tossing every few seconds. Transfer on a plate.

► Dry roast dried coconut in the same pan for 1 minute. Transfer into a large bowl.

► Add the puffed lotus seeds in the same pan and roast till crisp. Transfer into a bowl.

► Heat oil in the same pan on low heat.

► Add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, chaat masala, dried mango powder and black salt and mix well. Cook for 1 minute.

► Add the puffed lotus seeds and the roasted nuts and seeds and mix till well combined.

► Add powdered sugar and mix till well combined. Allow to cool completely.

► Take the bowl with dried coconut, add dried figs, raisins and roasted mixture, and chocolate chips. Mix till well combined.

► Transfer into an air tight container and serve as required.

Calories:

► 140

Proteins:

► 4 grams
